Welcome to Scriptoria!

The Grand Archive of Scriptoria is a shining beacon of knowledge; a place where scholars, mages, and historians from across the realm gather to study the near-endless texts within its walls. However, not all books are harmless. Within the forbidden section of the archive, something has gotten loose, and now the Head Archivist has called for aid to stop the spreading chaos.

You are a Seeker: a specialist sent by the Guild to uncover the source of the corruption and restore balance to the Archive before its knowledge is lost forever.

How to Play

Read the text to follow the story. Type a command and press Submit or Enter. For choices, type the number of the option you want.



Commands

  • Move - change locations
  • Search - look for items to pick up or people to speak to
  • Talk - speak with people in the room with you
  • Pick Up - add items to inventory
  • Inventory - view inventory
  • Use - consume or interact with an item in your inventory
  • Help - display this list of commands
